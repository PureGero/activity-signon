Sport Selection by Ethan Bulmer

First-in, first-served student sport selection website

Students log into the website during a set opening window and enrol into the sport of their choice

Selection Made Easy

  • Students are sent an email containing a link to log into the site
  • Student see the list of sports, enrol into the one they want, and they're done
  • First in first served selection process
  • Customised themes for each school to make students feel at home
  • Works on mobile devices

Simple Administration

  • Simple, cascading design for intuitive use
  • Restrict sports by year level and financial status
  • Bulk import student emails from IDAttend
  • Bulk export sport selections to csv
  • Selections can also be made for teachers

Built with Node.js

  • Front end built with HTML/CSS/JS
  • Back end built with Node.js
  • Designed to be fast and infinitely scalable

Powered by AWS

  • Highly secure datacenter
  • Designed for redundancy
  • Scales instantly to meet any demand
